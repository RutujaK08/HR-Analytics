📊 HR Analytics Dashboard
An interactive HR Analytics Dashboard developed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, and work-life balance. The dashboard enables HR teams to monitor key workforce metrics and identify factors contributing to employee turnover.

🎯 Project Objective
To provide data-driven insights into employee attrition and workforce trends, helping organizations improve employee retention and HR decision-making.

📁 Dataset Overview
Total Employees: 1,480
Features: 38 employee-related attributes
Key Dimensions:
Department
Job Role
Gender
Age Group
Salary Slab
Job Satisfaction
Work Life Balance
Attrition Status

📌 Dashboard Features
KPI Cards
Total Employees
Attrition Rate
Average Hourly Rate
Average Job Satisfaction
Average Work-Life Balance
Interactive Analysis
Department & Job Role Attrition Analysis
Salary Slab Wise Attrition
Age Group Wise Attrition
Attrition vs Job Satisfaction
Attrition vs Work-Life Balance
Interactive Elements
Department Filter
Gender Filter
Navigation Buttons
Download Report Button

📈 Key Insights
Employees aged 18–25 showed the highest attrition rate.
Lower salary slabs experienced higher employee turnover.
Attrition decreased as job satisfaction increased.
Better work-life balance was associated with lower attrition rates.
Sales and Research & Development departments showed higher attrition compared to other departments.
